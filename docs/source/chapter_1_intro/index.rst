Introduction
============

The BlackPill I/O Carrier
-------------------------

The **BlackPill I/O Carrier** is an expansion prototyping board for the STM32F411 BlackPill module, designed to bridge the gap between low-voltage microcontrollers and 24V industrial environments.    

.. image:: /images/hardware/01_BlackPillInterface/BlackPillInterface_3D.jpg
   :alt: 3D View of the BlackPill Interface Board
   :align: center
   :width: 600px


Key Development Pillars
-----------------------

The development of this **Evaluation Carrier Board** focuses on providing a versatile prototyping platform for laboratory environments. It is designed as a sub-assembly to facilitate the research and development of industrial-style interfaces.

* **Industrial-Level Prototyping**
  Integration of robust 24V-tolerant digital inputs to allow safe interfacing with common sensors during the early stages of system design.

* **Signal Evaluation**
  Specialized OpAmp stages are included to enable the testing and calibration of analog or PWM output signals in experimental setups.

* **Modular Connectivity**
  The layout is optimized for wireless evaluation (e.g., via HC-05 Bluetooth modules) to allow remote data monitoring without physical wiring.

* **Application Versatility**
  Serves as a flexible foundation for custom tools and research projects, such as developing experimental Digital Readouts (DROs) for machine tool applications.


Note: This board is an **Evaluation Kit** intended for use in a laboratory or development environment by qualified personnel. It is not a finished appliance and has not undergone EMC testing. Compliance with local regulations is the responsibility of the integrator.
